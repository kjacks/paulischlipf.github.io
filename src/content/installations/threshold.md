# Threshold

Sensors embedded in the floor track visitor movement, triggering layered audio and shifts in the overhead lighting grid. No two experiences of the space are identical — each body becomes a performer, each path a composition.

The work questions the boundary between audience and author. The factory's industrial history is woven into the soundscape through field recordings made on-site before demolition began.
