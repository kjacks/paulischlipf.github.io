# Field Study III

Taken across eighteen months at the same location, these photographs capture incremental transformations invisible to a single visit. The cliff face becomes a document — a record of weather, season, and slow collapse.

Printed at 1:1 scale with the original negative, each image preserves details invisible to the naked eye at the time of exposure.
