# Untitled No. 1

This piece investigates the dialogue between organic and synthetic materials. Reclaimed timber panels are embedded with poured resin voids, creating windows that distort and reflect the surrounding environment.

The tension between decay and preservation runs throughout — the wood is unfinished, its grain exposed, while the resin is polished to a mirror clarity.
